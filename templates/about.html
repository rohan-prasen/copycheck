About US

CopyCheck is a web application designed to help you analyze text content for potential duplicate or copied information. Simply upload a .txt or .docx file, and CopyCheck will process the text and provide you with feedback regarding potential copied content.

Our goal is to assist users in identifying and addressing instances of duplicate content, promoting originality and integrity in written work.

CopyCheck is powered by advanced algorithms that analyze the structure and patterns of text to identify similarities. However, it's important to note that the tool is not foolproof and may require human judgment for accurate assessment.

Thank you for using CopyCheck!
